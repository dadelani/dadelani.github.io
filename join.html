Get involved

Join the lab

We welcome students and collaborators who want to make AI work for every language, especially those it has historically left behind.

PhD students

Apply to the School of Computer Science at McGill and to Mila, and mention Prof. Adelani as a potential supervisor. Strong background in NLP, ML or speech is a plus.

Masters students

McGill and Mila MSc applicants interested in multilingual NLP, low-resource languages or speech are encouraged to reach out.

Interns & visitors

We host interns from around the world for focused research projects. Include your CV, interests and a short note on why this lab.

Before you email

A few tips

  • Read a couple of our recent papers and mention what excites you.
  • Tell us which language(s) or problem you'd like to work on.
  • Attach your CV and a brief description of relevant experience.
  • Native speakers of under-served languages are especially encouraged to apply.
